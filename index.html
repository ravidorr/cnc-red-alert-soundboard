About Red Alert

Command & Conquer: Red Alert was released on October 31, 1996 by Westwood Studios and published by Virgin Interactive. Set in an alternate timeline where Einstein prevents World War II by eliminating Hitler, the game pits the Allied Forces against the Soviet Union in a classic real-time strategy showdown.

Red Alert became one of the most influential RTS games ever made, selling over 10 million copies worldwide. The game's memorable unit voices - especially Tanya's one-liners like "Shake It Baby" and "Let's Rock," plus the satisfying "Acknowledged" and "Affirmative" responses - have become iconic in gaming culture and popular internet memes.

In 2020, EA released a Remastered Collection with updated graphics, but this soundboard preserves the original 1996 audio - the authentic sounds that 90s gamers remember. Westwood Studios was later acquired by EA in 1998, but their legacy lives on through these classic C&C Red Alert sounds.
